ABOUT THE VIBRANT INDIA FAIR

Vibrant India Fair is a grand celebration of India's rich cultural heritage, craftsmanship, and innovation. As one of the most anticipated events of the year, our fair serves as a platform to showcase the best of India's art, traditions, and modern advancements.


HOME WARE

A complete line of homewares including kitchenware, kitchen appliances, cookware, thermoware, tableware, earthenware, bar accessories, bathroom accessories, cleaning and maintenance products, plastic & storage items, home appliances safety & security, disposable articles, and general household products.

HOME TEXTILES

Textiles such as furniture & decorative fabrics, curtains & blinds, decorative made ups, mattress & pillows, comforters & blankets, towels & bath linen, table & kitchen linen, rugs & carpets, etc.

HOME DECOR

Decorative wallpapers, wall decorations, blinds, wooden or synthetic flooring, artificial grass, rugs & carpets, bathroom accessories, handicrafts, figurines, table decorations, decorative hardware, clocks, decorative lights & fans, decorative accessories, dry flowers and fragrances, artificial flowers and fauna, aroma oils, candles and candle stands.

HOME FURNITURE

A comprehensive selection of living room, dining room, bedroom, kitchen, study room, children's room, bathroom, home office furniture, accent furniture, outdoor & leisure furniture. Furniture Such As Sofas, Chairs, Dining Tables, Beds, Wardrobes, Coffee Tables, Console Tables, Cabinets, Side Tables, Nesting Tables, Wall Separators, Chairs, Bean Bags & Pouffes, Rocking Chairs & Stools, Kids' Furniture, Garden Furniture, Racks & Trolleys, Bar Cabinets & Storage Chests, Dressers & Mirrors, Wine Racks, Glass Holders, Book Shelves & Magazine Holders, Showcases And Display Units Are All Examples Of Items We Offer.

GIFTS

Awards And Rewards, Handmade Crafts, Home Decor, Kitchen Appliances, Stationery, Office Supplies, Innovative Gift Boxes, Luxury Gifts And Lifestyle Products, Gold & Silver Gifts, Luxury Gifts And Lifestyle Products, And Celebration & Festive Gifts.

LOCKING SOLUTION

Mortise Locks, Cylindrical Locks, Deadbolts, Mortise Cylinders, Rim Cylinders, Key-in-knob Cylinders/key-in-lever Cylinders, Exit Devices, High-security Locks, Access Control Locks, Biometric Locks, Safe Locks, Magnetic Locks.

MODULAR KITCHEN
