Planning Your Event

When Do You Start Designing Your Floor Plan?

Before selling sponsorship packages, you must know how many booths you can offer and their price points. During your brainstorming stage, review how many exhibitor packages you sold at your last show.

  • Send a post-show survey to previous exhibitors to know if they plan on returning.
  • Ask if they want to purchase a bigger booth or request new features.
  • Estimate attendee & exhibitor count to decide venue space.
  • Adjust your rented space after registration slows down.
  • Finalize your layout process with the new numbers.

These steps ensure you maximize exhibitor satisfaction, optimize space, and boost event revenue with a well-planned floor design.
