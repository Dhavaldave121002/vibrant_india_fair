VENUE:Chennai Trade Center

The July 2025 edition of Vibrant India 2025 will be held at Chennai Trade Center, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. The venue is strategically located in the heart of Chennai, making it easily accessible for both domestic and international visitors. Known for its modern facilities and spacious exhibition halls, Chennai Trade Center provides an ideal setting for showcasing the latest innovations and trends in the industry.
