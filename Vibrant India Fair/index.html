About the Show

Premier Stage for Industry Innovation

the Show

Gifts World Expo, South India's largest gifting exhibition, brings together exhibitors and visitors on a global platform to explore customized gifts, promotional solutions, and innovative concepts. Designed for the B2B landscape, it empowers businesses to generate leads, discover trends, and gain valuable insights. Today, this leading platform is driving growth within the gifting sector. Ekta Shree

ASM - Samsonite

We are a luggage bag company and have been leading this industry for over 100 years. We have been taking part in the Gifts World Expo for the past 3 years, and we also exhibit in Delhi and Bangalore.
